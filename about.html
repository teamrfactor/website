About us

We are a team of 23 young enthusiastic robotics students from Mumbai, India. Many of us have been working with LEGO® robots, World Robot Olympiads, and the FIRST® programs since 2009. We’ve represented the country at several international events, tallying a total of 43 international selections amongst us. Our participation in national and international competitions has helped us grow into innovators and leaders. It has imparted to us important virtues like patience and compassion, and has developed and enriched us as individuals and as a team, making us efficient planners and organizers.
